Great gastronomic experiences at the Vranov reservoir

Our dishes are prepared from high-quality fresh ingredients. Both steak lovers and vegetarians will eat to their liking here. In the summer, our menu includes fresh fish. Every Tuesday we organize a musical evening with a barbecue. Our extensive selection of wine made by South-Moravian winemakers will not let you down at any time during the year. Food is served in the modern interior of the restaurant or on the spacious terrace with a view of the reservoir. Great gastronomic experiences at the Vranov reservoir
