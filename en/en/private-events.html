Company and private events at the Vranov reservoir

Whether you come to the Vranov reservoir for team-building or to celebrate an important life event, the GAUDEO Guesthouse will bring you unforgettable experiences. We will put you up, arrange catering and music and make sure that everything runs smoothly.

Bigger events are held in 2 reconstructed and fully equipped rooms. They can be used for training, conferences and gatherings, as well as for wedding receptions or birthday parties.

Catering and buffet 

We will make sure to provide you with a great gastronomic experience. We will prepare refreshments during the day, a lunch menu and a buffet supper. In the summer months, we can grill fish, poultry and pork steaks or vegetables.

Looking for something extra? Would you like to jazz up your event? We can organize a bartender’s show, a cocktail party or a wine-tasting event.

Inquire about catering

Weddings and celebrations in a beautiful countryside location 

Are you planning your wedding day and looking for a remarkable and romantic place? Our restaurant and guesthouse meet both. Surrounded by unspoilt nature, a stone’s throw away from the Vranov reservoir.

We will provide a comprehensive service – music, decoration and a big reception. Suites overlooking the reservoir will be at your disposal. The wedding ceremony can take place directly on the beach, on board a pleasure boat, or on the magnificent premises of the Vranov Chateau.

Inquire about a wedding or celebration

Team-building around the Vranov reservoir

Accommodation in the GAUDEO Guesthouse will be the starting point of your adventure as well as a place that you will like to return to in order to recharge your batteries after a hectic day. 

The environs of the guesthouse offer any amount of team-building and outdoor activities. From cycling to water sports, fishing, wine up to historical sights. The most significant ones include the Vranov Chateau, the ruins of the Cornštejn Castle and the Bítov Castle.

Inquire about team-building
