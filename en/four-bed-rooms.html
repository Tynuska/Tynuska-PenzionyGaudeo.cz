Four-bed rooms

The suites are located where the action is. The guesthouse is situated next to a camp, there are many restaurants, a beach and a number of popular tourist destinations in the vicinity. Despite that, there is no need to worry about noise. When you close the windows, your suite becomes a quiet retreat to recharge your batteries.

In the morning, you will wake up to the lovely smell of a warm breakfast and delicious coffee from the guesthouse restaurant. The buffet offers something to everyone. It goes without saying that everything is included in the price.
