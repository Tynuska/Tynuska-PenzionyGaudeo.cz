Spacious suites overlooking the Vranov reservoir

Your holiday on the Vranov beach starts in the GAUDEO Guesthouse. View our suites. 

Select a room and book your stay

You can choose from 32 suites located in 3 interconnected buildings. Each suite comes with a view of the beach and its own balcony or front garden.

Double rooms

Suites for couples, individuals or friends. The rooms have all the mod cons and are fully equipped for your stay (29 m2). Wi-Fi comes as standard, the kitchenette is fully equipped. And in case you forget your hair dryer or any other devices, you can find them in the room or borrow from us.

Four-bed rooms

Spacious suites for a family or a group of friends (39 m2). The rooms have all the mod cons and are fully equipped. En suite bathroom, kitchenette, fridge and Wi-Fi will be fully at your disposal. But you will hardly have time to browse the internet. In the environs of Vranov, there is so much to do all the time.

Information about accommodation:

  • Prices include VAT.
  • The price includes a buffet breakfast and access to the campsite.
  • Bed linen, final cleaning and parking for 1 car per room are not included.
  • A child under 4 years old always stays free of charge without bed and board.
  • Baby cot free of charge.
  • The price does not include a fee (holiday 20 CZK person / night from 18 to 70 years).
  • For TOP season we preferably accept reservations for the whole week.
